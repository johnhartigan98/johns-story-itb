Fauna

The surrounding ecology of the itb campus is rich with fauna.The campus green areas such as the field on the hill and the hedgerows surrounding the parameter of the campus. In the field there are mainly rabbits and hares but there are also a large array of birds that are living in the hedgerow aswell.

Flora

The flora of the ITB campus has a wide arrray of plantlife and is mainly made up of oak and ash. The field is rich with flowers and weeds that help the fauna to thrive on the campus. The trees on the campus form hedgerows which give the fauna protection. Because of this ITB has a wide biodiversity which most campuses wouldnt usually have.
